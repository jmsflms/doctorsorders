Noise and curfews

Please don’t be noisy in the garden or hot tub after midnight on Fridays and Saturdays, or after 10:30pm on weekdays.

The cinema room, dining room and games room are good places for late-night chats.

Hot tub

The hot tub is cleaned and treated before and after every visit. It is left cooler between stays, so allow an hour or two for it to warm up.

  • Use the arrows beside the display to adjust the temperature.
  • The other buttons control the jets and lights.
  • Please shower off sun lotion, moisturiser, make-up and excess soap first.
  • Never take glass into the hot tub. Plastic glasses are provided.
  • If the water falls below the jets, use the hose to top it up—unless a hosepipe ban is in force.

Testing strips, de-foamer and a small water vacuum are available. Never put the vacuum’s charging connection in the water unless it is fully sealed.

Check South East Water restrictions

Cinema room

The wall buttons raise and lower the projector screen. The small black Amazon Fire TV remote should control both the Fire TV and projector; its blue button turns them on.

Please leave all remotes in the cinema room. The large sofa can also be used as an extra bed.

Heating and hot water

The house has gas central heating, assisted by solar thermal panels. The Nest thermostat is the small circular display in the ground-floor hallway.

Each room has a thermostatic radiator and an electric fan heater. Please contact us before touching the commercial boiler.

Doors, windows and garden lighting

To lock the garden door, lift the handle while turning the key. Please lock doors and close windows when leaving valuables in the house.

The garden lights normally come on around sunset and switch off at 10pm. Their power supply is in the larger garden shed, but you should not normally need to access it.

Rubbish and recycling

The silver kitchen bin is for general waste. Please put rubbish inside the two bin liners rather than directly into the bin.

Large bags are provided for glass and paper. If anything becomes full or smelly, the outside bins are at the front of the house, to the right.

Collection is on Monday. If you leave Sunday evening or Monday morning, putting the bins out would be very helpful, but it is not required.

Breakages and stains

Accidents happen. Please tell us about breakages or stains so we know what needs fixing or replacing.

Please don’t jump on the beds, and be especially careful with red wine near the white bedding. Stain removers are in the laundry room.

Wine glasses have surprisingly short lives here. If there is a full-scale wine-glass massacre, we would appreciate replacements.

Towels, laundry and supplies

Indoor and outdoor towels are provided. To save energy, we appreciate guests bringing their own when convenient.

We provide an initial supply of toilet paper, kitchen roll and cleaning products. Please don’t take our towels home.

The washing machine adds detergent and conditioner automatically. We recommend TurboWash with the spin set to 1400.

Feeding a crowd

Kitchen

Milk: We don’t provide milk because the house is sometimes empty for several days.
Coffee machine

The machine is mainly for making large pots of filter coffee. Labels on the machine will guide you. The reusable filter can be rinsed or put in the dishwasher; no paper filter is needed.

The espresso function is best used by people familiar with this type of machine. You will need to bring your preferred espresso coffee.

Range cooker, gas hobs and ovens

If the cooker hasn’t been used recently, the gas can take a while to reach it. Please persevere. The built-in spark works, and lighters are also kept nearby.

The gas ovens light using the control knobs.

Electric oven

The left knob selects the type of heat and the right knob controls temperature. The oven tends to run cool, so check the independent thermometer inside. As a general guide, add about 20°C.

Microwave

For simple heating, repeatedly press the small diamond button to add 30 seconds.

Air fryer

The large air fryer is excellent for cooking for a group.

Before you go

Check-out

These few things make a big difference to the cleaning team. Thank you.

  • Strip used beds and leave the linen on each bed.
  • Put used towels in a bath.
  • Load and start the dishwasher.
  • Take rubbish to the bins at the side of the house.
  • Turn off the lights.
  • Turn down the hot tub temperature.
  • Return the key to the lockbox.
